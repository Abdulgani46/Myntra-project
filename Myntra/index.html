About Us

Our Mission

  • Provide customers with a convenient and enjoyable shopping experience for fashion and lifestyle products.
  • Offer a diverse range of high-quality products from leading brands at competitive prices.
  • Continuously innovate and adapt to meet the evolving needs and preferences of our customers.
  • Build long-lasting relationships with customers based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service.
  • Contribute positively to the fashion industry and society as a whole through responsible business practices.

Our Vision

  • Become the leading online destination for fashion and lifestyle products, known for our unparalleled selection, convenience, and customer service.
  • Empower individuals to express their unique style and personality through our diverse range of products and personalized shopping experiences.
  • Drive innovation in the fashion industry by leveraging technology and data analytics to anticipate and fulfill customer needs.
  • Forge strong partnerships with brands, designers, and suppliers to deliver the latest trends and exclusive collections to our customers.
  • Strive to create a positive impact on the environment and society by promoting sustainable practices and ethical sourcing.

Our Story

  • Founding: Myntra was founded in 2007 by Mukesh Bansal, Ashutosh Lawania, and Vineet Saxena as an online platform for personalized gift items.
  • Transition to Fashion: In 2010, Myntra pivoted to focus exclusively on fashion and lifestyle products, capitalizing on the growing e-commerce market in India.
  • Growth and Expansion: Over the years, Myntra experienced rapid growth, expanding its product offerings, partnering with top brands, and introducing innovative features like personalized recommendations and virtual try-ons.
  • Acquisition by Flipkart: In 2014, Myntra was acquired by Flipkart, India's leading e-commerce marketplace, further solidifying its position in the online fashion industry.
  • Leadership in Fashion: Today, Myntra is recognized as India's largest fashion and lifestyle e-tailer, serving millions of customers with a wide range of products and services.
